# Hone

I've recently been interested in learning to become a slightly better poker player. One big part of getting
better at poker is understanding poker ranges, and learning GTO—game theory optimal—play for specific positions.

There are very sophisticated tools like [GTO Wizard](https://gtowizard.com/) that are clearly the **best** tool
for the job. But I just wanted something lightweight that I could use on-the-go to get a little bit better
before my next casual game with friends.

And so I build Hone. Think of it as "tinder for poker decisions." Instead of trying to account for all the real
nuance that's involved in learning to be a great poker player, Hone just gives you simple scenarios and lets you
pick from clear options like call/raise/fold. It then uses a spaced-repetition algorithm that's usually meant for
flashcards to show you hands that you got wrong more often than hands you got right.

That's pretty much it.

You can track some stats, and focus on table position. Eventually I'll let you select your stack and table size.
Maybe even more, we'll see.

Anyway, Hone is completely free on the app store. Try it out!

## Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 19, 2026*

### Overview

Hone is a fully offline poker training app. Your training data, statistics, and settings are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to any server.

### Data We Store Locally

All data remains on your device:

- Training history and review events
- Spaced repetition card states
- Statistics (ELO rating, accuracy, streaks)
- Your settings and preferences

This data is stored in a local SQLite database within the app sandbox. It is not accessible to other apps and is removed if you uninstall Hone.

### Network Activity

Hone connects to the internet only to check for over-the-air (OTA) app updates via Expo's update service. This may transmit basic device metadata (platform, app version, runtime version) to determine whether an update is available. No personal data or training data is sent.

### Data Collection Summary

- Personal information collected: none
- Training data shared with third parties: none
- Advertising or analytics tracking: none
- Account or login required: no

### Data Export

You can export your training history at any time from the Settings screen in CSV or JSON format. This data stays on your device until you choose to share it.

### Children's Privacy

Hone does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not collect any personal information from any user.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app with an updated revision date.
